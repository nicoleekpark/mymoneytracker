## [1.0.0] - Pending App Store Review

Initial release.

### Features

- **Dashboard** - Monthly, yearly, and all-time views
- **Calendar View** - Visual spending patterns with daily breakdown
- **Transactions** - Track expenses, income, and transfers
- **Categories** - 20+ categories with subcategories
- **Multiple Accounts** - Cash, checking, savings, credit cards
- **Net Worth Tracking** - Assets, liabilities, balance sheet
- **Budget Alerts** - Get notified when approaching limits
- **Quick Entry** - Customizable chips for frequent transactions
- **Drafts** - Save incomplete transactions, finish later
- **Dark Mode** - Full dark theme support

### Privacy

- Fully offline - no cloud, no sync
- No account required
- No data collection
- Everything stays on device
