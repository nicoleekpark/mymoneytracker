## [1.0.0] - 2024-XX-XX

Initial release.

### Features
- Dashboard with monthly, yearly, and all-time views
- Transaction management (expense, income, transfer)
- Category and subcategory tracking
- Multiple account support (cash, checking, savings, credit)
- Budget alerts
- Quick-entry chips for frequent transactions
- Draft transactions (save and continue later)
- Spending insights and trends
- Dark mode support
- Fully offline - no account required, no data collection
