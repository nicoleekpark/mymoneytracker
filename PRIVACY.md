# Privacy Policy for MyMoneyTracker

**Last Updated: June 19, 2026**

## Overview

MyMoneyTracker is designed with privacy as a core principle. Your financial data stays on your device and is never transmitted to us or any third party.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect any data.**

- No personal information
- No financial data
- No usage analytics
- No advertising identifiers
- No cookies or tracking

## Data Storage

All data you enter in MyMoneyTracker is stored locally on your device only. We have no access to your data because it never leaves your device.

## Third-Party Services

MyMoneyTracker does not use any third-party services that collect data. There are:

- No analytics SDKs
- No advertising networks
- No cloud storage services
- No bank connections or financial data aggregators

## Data Sharing

We do not share any data because we do not have access to any of your data.

## Data Security

Your data is protected by your device's built-in security features. Since data never leaves your device, there is no transmission risk.

## Children's Privacy

MyMoneyTracker does not collect any data from any users, including children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last Updated" date above.
