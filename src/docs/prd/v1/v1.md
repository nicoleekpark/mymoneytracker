## 1. Overview

### Product Description
HoH Finance Tracker is a cross-platform personal finance application built with Expo, React Native, and Tamagui, supporting iOS, Android, and web.

The app is offline-first, uses `expo-sqlite` for local persistence with a custom migration system, and focuses on **manual, intentional financial tracking** rather than automatic bank sync.

The core design inspiration is Google Calendar: date-centric navigation, clear temporal context, and fast visual understanding of activity over time.

## 5. v1 Feature Scope

### 5.1 Dashboard Tab

The Dashboard is the primary entry point and contains a **large toggle control** with four modes:

- Overview (default)
- Cash Flow (placeholder)
- Accounts
- Net Worth

---

### 5.1.1 Overview

#### Time Controls
- Month view (default: current month)
- Year view (default: current year)
- All (entire year summary)
- Adaptive date picker based on selected view

#### Budget & Spending
- Single global monthly budget
- Display:
  - Budgeted amount
  - Actual spent amount
- If spending exceeds budget:
  - Calendar date when budget is crossed is visually marked

#### Daily Cash Flow Calendar (Month View)
- Month calendar grid
- Each day displays:
  - Total expense
  - Total income
- Toggle:
  - Show expense
  - Show income

#### Monthly Spending by Category
- Donut chart of spending by category
- Total spending shown in center
- Clicking a category:
  - Shows top 5 subcategories
- Clicking center:
  - Resets to all categories
- Below chart:
  - Top 5 subcategories overall

## 8. Account & Money Semantics

### Account Model
- Accounts have:
  - nature: asset | liability
  - kind:
    - cash
    - checking
    - savings
    - credit_card
    - loan
    - investment
    - other

### Credit Cards
- Credit cards are liability accounts
- Spending:
  - Expense on credit card account
- Paying off credit cards:
  - Transfer from asset account to credit card account
